About VirFolio

VirFolio is a comprehensive virtual portfolio tracking platform designed to help investors monitor their investments across US and Indian stock markets.

Our Mission

To provide investors with a powerful, intuitive, and free platform for tracking their investment portfolios, analyzing performance, and making informed investment decisions.

Key Features

  • Real-time stock prices from US and Indian markets
  • Multiple portfolio management
  • Performance analytics and visualizations
  • Sector and geographical allocation tracking
  • Historical performance tracking
  • Multi-currency support (USD and INR)

Technology Stack

Backend

  • Flask (Python)
  • SQLAlchemy ORM
  • yfinance API

Frontend

  • Tailwind CSS
  • DaisyUI Components
  • Plotly Charts
